Our Services

These are the services that we offer

Learner Driver

This is an education course to educate candidates on Road rules, signs and driver responsibilities to prepare learner drivers for the Learners Licence(LL) test
There is a training facility to practice K53 manoeuvres

Driving lessons

WE offer driving lessons for the following codes:
- Code B - Light Motor Vehicle
- Code EB
- Light motor vehicle towing a trailer exceeding 750Kg

Refresher driver training

The Refresher driver training course is for:
- Candidates who have not driven for a while due to whatever reasons and need to get back behind the wheel
- Assists in reminding them about the K53 manoeuvres

Simulator Training

Simulator training :
- for absolute beginners to learn the K53 procedures

Upgrading of licence codes

- We offer upgrading of licence codes
- eg. from light motor vehicle to heavy motor vehicle

Instructor Training Courses

- The Instructor Training Courses is for:
- candidates who want to become a light motor vehicle driving instructor
